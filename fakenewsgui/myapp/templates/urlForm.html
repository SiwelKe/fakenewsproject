DETECTING FAKE NEWS USING NAIVE BAYES CLASSIFIERS




ABOUT THIS PROJECT
This artificial intelligence can predict whether a news article is fake or real based on the words used in the article. Some of the algorithms used are the Naive Bayes Family such as :

1.Bernoulli Naive Bayes
2.Multinomial Naive Bayes
3.Gaussian Naive Bayes classifier


HOW TO USE ?
Enter a URL in the box above, the bot will load the article, extract the visible text, and use different Naive Bayes classifier models to make a prediction of whether the article is fake or real. This may take several seconds because the URL you enter has to be loaded and processed.

TYPES OF ARTICLES?
The model has been trained only on a dataset that tried to cover vast topics and thus it's likely to produce credible results for any domain.Any news article is acceptable.
